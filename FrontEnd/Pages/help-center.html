Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I sign up for a course?

To sign up for a course, go to the "Courses" section, choose the course you're interested in, and click on the "Enroll" button. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration process.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets like PayPal. All transactions are processed securely through our payment gateways.

3. How can I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you will receive instructions to create a new password.

4. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email at support@yourlearningplatform.com or by phone at +91 123 456 7890. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any issues.

5. What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy allows you to request a refund within 14 days of purchase if you are not satisfied with the course. Please refer to our refund policy page for more detailed information.

6. How can I provide feedback?

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback through the "Contact Us" form on our website or by sending us an email at feedback@yourlearningplatform.com.
